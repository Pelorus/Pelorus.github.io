Why an MVP?

A Minimum Viable Product is a proof-of-concept for your idea. It demonstrates your potential to investors, developers, potential co-founders, and friends. Having an MVP gives you the opportunity to collect real user feedback about your idea and strategies before you think about investing time or money in a development team or bringing on co-founders.
